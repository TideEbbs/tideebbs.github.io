Welcome!

Irish Wrecks Online is a repository of information for novice, amateur, and advanced divers seeking information regarding the various shipwrecks surrounding the Irish coast. Within this website you can find:

  • A plethora of illustrations of the ship types found on the Irish Coast

  • Clearly Marked Reference maps for each documented wreck, ordered both by county and name

  • A regular set of update notes for each time the site's information is adjusted

  • A repository of technical language and jargon used by experienced ship wreck divers, as well as other terms used on the site

  • References to all other sites used as reference for our own, as well as additional sources such as books and journals.

Feel free to link to our site for others who may need our site's services!
